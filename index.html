How to Play Dawblins

🎯 Objective

One player describes a monster image while the others draw it. Try to recreate the monster as accurately as possible!

🎮 Game Setup

  • Choose your viewing time (how long to study the monster)
  • Choose your drawing time (how long to describe and draw)
  • Use the touch picker to decide who goes first

📝 How to Play

  1. Study Phase: The describer studies the monster image
  2. Drawing Phase: The describer tells the team what to draw while they sketch
  3. Reveal: Compare your drawings with the original!

💡 Tips for Describers

  • Start with basic shapes and overall structure
  • Be specific about placement and proportions
  • Use simple, clear language
  • Guide step-by-step, don't rush

🎮 Game Rules

  • Each round, a different player should be the describer
  • No peeking at the monster during drawing phase!
  • Describers can't draw or gesture - only words!
  • Have fun and be creative with your descriptions!

🏆 Scoring Ideas

  • Rate each drawing from 1-10 for accuracy
  • Award points for specific features captured
  • Vote for the most creative interpretation
  • Keep track of rounds won by each team
